Technology

Obvioulsly technology is forever changing and adapting to the new world of today. There are a countless number of trends to be aware of but i think the most important one is AI (artificail intelligence). As more money is put into developing new AI sytemts and bots, it can stand to reason that, in the near future, jobs we know today will be completely automated. This will change the workforce drastically. Chat bots are very common right now, I assume they will be the sole communication between customers for certain companies. With 24/7 access to problem solving software, chat bots will be able to eliminate the need for multiple customer service representivies and possibly even supervising management.
