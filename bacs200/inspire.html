Idina Menzel

What makes a hero? A hero is someone that we look up to. Heroes embody what we truly value most. Therefore who we select as role models says a lot about who we are as people.

The heroes that I most admire have the following characteristics:

  • Outrageously Talented - she is a mezzo-soprano who has broken into film, theatre, and television
  • Loving Mother - has one son
  • Own Show - If/Then is a broadway show based on her life (which she also starred in)
  • Commitment - She started her career in 1995 and is still going strong

After 25 years, idina Menzel has stolen the hearts of the people. She is most famous for her role in Disney's Frozen and the lead role in Broadway's Wicked. After ruling broadway, Idina decided she wanted more. Throughout her career she has inspired millions of people with her heartfelt characters and shows.
